Amazon QR Label Maker

Scan the QR code to create a shipping label for Amazon.




Instructions

  1. Enter or scan the Amazon QR Code in the text box above.
  2. Click the Generate Label button.
  3. The label will be copied to your clipboard.
  4. Click the Open Labelary button to open Labelar in a new tab.
  5. Paste the label into the Labelary viewer.
  6. Click redrew to get the new label.
  7. Download the label as a PDF.
